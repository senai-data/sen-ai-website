Design tokens

One source of truth for colour, type, spacing, radius and depth. Everything downstream - components, the agency dashboard, the scan-results pages - is built from these and nothing else. Coral stays as the primary brand asset; the rest is tightened into a real scale.

Colour ramps

Two ramps only. Coral carries brand and primary action. A cool-charcoal neutral carries 90% of the surface - sober and trustworthy, the way any procurement or brand team expects an audit tool to look, in any vertical.

Semantic roles

Components reference these names, never raw hexes. Re-skinning for white-label agencies means swapping the role, not hunting inline classes across 35 pages.

Status & severity

Status roles are categorical (one of six). Severity is an ordered scale of five with a deliberate perceptual escalation - grey to blue to amber to orange to red - so a crisis reads as more urgent than a warning at a glance.

Type scale

System stack only (no new licence). A data-dense 14px body anchors the scale, matching the reference density of Ahrefs and Linear. Tight tracking on headings, generous line-height on body.

Spacing

A 4px-based scale. The current p-4 / p-5 / p-6 / p-8 chaos collapses to two component rules: cards pad at space-5 (24px), compact controls at space-3 (12px). Everything else snaps to the scale.

Radius

Five steps, each with one job. The mixed rounded-md / lg / xl usage maps to: inputs & chips = md, cards = lg, modals = xl, pills & avatars = full.

Shadow tiers

Real depth hierarchy replaces shadow-sm-on-everything. Flat cards rest on a border (xs), interactive surfaces lift on hover (sm to md), overlays float (lg to xl).
